AI Breakthrough: Machines Now Learning Like Humans

Disadvantages of AI

Nowadays, AI made its way into classrooms as a shortcut for some students. They create papers on any topic in minutes, tempting those near deadlines or struggling with concepts. These programs can recreate writing styles and format citations, making them look real. However, relying on AI for assignments is a disadvantagous choice. Firstly, it rejects the true purpose of education. Assignments are made to enhance students' thinking, research skills, and the ability to explain their understanding of the topics. Skipping these steps with AI leads to a backlash from knowledge and worsens the development of important academic abilities.
